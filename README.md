# FairPath AI

FairPath AI is a multi-agent AI system that helps international students navigate the job search with more fairness, clarity, and confidence. Built for the **Avanade Challenge — AI Future of Work** at **AI Hackathon 2026**, FairPath AI addresses key barriers international students face, including hiring bias, sponsorship concerns, and policy uncertainty.

## The Problem
International students often face three major challenges:
- hidden identity bias in hiring
- employer hesitation around visa sponsorship
- stress from OPT deadlines and changing immigration policies

## Our Solution
FairPath AI acts as a support system that helps students:
- reduce bias in application materials
- match their skills to the right roles
- build a stronger case for sponsorship
- understand how policy and market changes affect their job search

## Key Agents
- **Privacy Guardian** — removes identity-linked bias and masks sensitive information
- **Career Architect** — matches student skills to job roles and explains skill gaps
- **Sponsorship Strategist** — shows the ROI of sponsoring international talent
- **Resilience Coach** — provides “what-if” guidance to reduce uncertainty
- **Policy Sentry** — tracks policy and market changes affecting students
- **Compliance Auditor** — monitors fairness and responsible AI use

## Tools & Data Sources
- O*NET API
- College Scorecard
- USCIS policy data
- Kaggle job market data
- AI Fairness 360

## Vision

FairPath AI is more than a job tool — it is a specialized advocate for global talent, designed to create fairer access to career opportunities for international students.
